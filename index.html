Autism Lunch Club

The Autism Lunch Club was established to provide a space for students with who experience difficulties with social interaction to come together in a group. This group allows students to meet like-minded others and share their experiences of University life.

This club gets so much positive feedback from prospective and current students, and is unique to the University of Huddersfield! So much so, we recently featured in a Guardian article, a big congrats to all involved!

Big White Wall Usage and Stats

Since the start of this academic term, there has been a total of 281 new registrations to Big White Wall!

 

This is a 55% increase in comparison to registrations for this first term compared to the Autumn term of 2018.

 

Further to this, there was a 15% increase in total logins across September to November for this year compared to last year.
